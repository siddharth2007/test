Sebastian Vettel

Sebastian Vettel is the one of most successful drivers in formula 1.
Sebastian Vettel holds the record for the youngest world champion. Sebastian won 4 Formula 1 championships from 2010-2013. Sebastian Vettel drove for RedBull Racing, Ferrari & currently drives for Aston Martin F1 Team
