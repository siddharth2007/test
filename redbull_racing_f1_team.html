RedBull Racing F1 Team

RedBull Racing F1 Team is a team formed by energy drinks company redbull. The team is based out in UK and has housed some of the most talented formula 1 drivers including Max Verstappen, Daniel Riccardo, and Sebastian vettel. Their most recent championship win was in 2021 and they are now competing with Ferrari to retain the title.
