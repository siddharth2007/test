Mercedes AMG F1 Team

Mercedes AMG F1 Team is a relatively new formula 1 team which came on the grid in 2010, bringing Michael Schumacher back from his sabbatical. The team is based out in the UK. The team is currently the strongest on the grid with the record for most consecutive world titles. Lewis Hamilton drives for the team alongside George Russell.
