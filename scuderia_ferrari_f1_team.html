Scuderia Ferrari F1 Team

Scuderia Ferrari F1 Team is the most successful and oldest f1 team in the sport with over 16 world titles. The team is based out of Italy. Drivers such as Michael Schumacher, Sebastian Vettel, Fernando Alonso drove for the team. Ferrari most recent championship win was in 2007 but the team is leading the ongoing championship battle in 2022.
